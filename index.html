About Us

New Vision is a media production company that is founded in Egypt. The founders have over 25 years of experience in video production and broadcasting in Egypt, Saudi Arabia, Dubai, and more countries inside and outside of the Middle East. They have worked with a vast array of clients from school events and weddings to major network television productions. You can trust our wealth of experience and technical expertise to give your company the best quality for the best value.

A Commitment To Your Vision

We will guide you through your project from pre-production to the final product. We will use our expertise and high-end equipment to give you the best production that will bring you the best results. Additionally, we use our knowledge of the industry to professionally broadcast your image and engage your audience with the best in-house AV equipment.

Our Services

From individual buyers and renters to small events to TV shows, we have it all. Here are some of the services we offer:

Pre-Production

Pre-Production is anything that happens before we actually start to shoot. Pre-Production allows us to become a part of your team. It gives us the opputunity to understand your goal, give creative input using our expertise, and establish a common goal that meets, and, hopefully, exceeds your expectations.
Our Pre-production Services include: Location Scouting, Casting and Talent Auditions, Budgeting, Scheduling, Production Management, and Hiring Crews for Shoots.

Production

After creating a plan and establishing a common goal, Production is the part where the fun begins because this where the shoot starts. In this part of the process, we will become your directors, videographers, light technicians, broadcasters, and more.
Our Production Services include: Producer, Director, Director of Photography, Videographer, Audio Technicians, Lighting Technicians, Production Assistants. We also provide all of the Film and Video equipment.

Post-Production

After we are done with the shoot, the Post-Production is where we take your footage and work with you to edit into a video that you would be proud of and one that will move, motivate, bring back memories, or entertain!
Our Post-Production Services include: Producer, Director, Editor, Editing Equipment, and we can help with all your Duplication and Packaging requirements. We can also help with animation and graphics. We can also help with Broadcasting using our own AV.

Equipment Rentals

Our team can provide you with any equipment you want. We have equipment for rent and for sale. We own some equipment, but our connections in Egypt, Dubai, and Saudi Arabia can provide you with any equipment you would want and for the best prices. For more info abbout what equipment we have access to, please contact us.
Our Equipment Categories include: Camera Bodies, Lenses, Camera Accessories, Camera Stabilizers, Grip, Lighting, Audio, Generators, Augmented Reality Devices, AV Equipment.

Don't hesitate to contact us if you need more info about the equipment we have or to hire us for your next project!
